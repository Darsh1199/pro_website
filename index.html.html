Our Story

Cooked with Tradition

Saffron House was born from a simple belief — that great food brings people together. Since 2012, we have been serving authentic Indian cuisine to families, friends, and food lovers across Pune.

Every dish on our menu is inspired by regional recipes from across India — from the spicy curries of Rajasthan to the gentle flavours of Kerala. We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients and traditional cooking methods to ensure every bite feels like home.
